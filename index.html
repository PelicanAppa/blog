Places To Visit

Pitlochry

I visited on October 2023

Pitlochry is one of Scotland's most beautiful and vibrant places to visit. In the heart of Scotland with real hospitality, clear sparkling air, beautiful scenery, rich clan history, fine food, plenty of space and lots to see and do. Pitlochry is primarily a holiday destination, which caters for the holiday maker year round in its own special way.

Loch Ness

I visited on August 2021

In this location, at the old Drumnadrochit Hotel, Mrs Aldie MacKay reported seeing a 'whale-like fish' or 'water beast' in the waters of Loch Ness. Her sighting sparked the birth of a monster media frenzy and created a global legend. Walk through 500 million years of history. Delve deeper into ancient Scottish myths, legends and scientific research. Uncover the mysteries of the loch and become part of the story in this unique tour.

Review On The Movies

Star Wars: Episode III - Rvenge of the Sith
If I had to choose a favorite from all the Star Wars films it would definitely be this one

Absolutely astounding and incredible. If I had to choose a favorite from just the first 3 Star Wars films it would definitely be this one, with its great mystery, musical score, characters, intense battles, astounding effects, wonderful locations and all else this film has to offer this masterpiece is yet another Star Wars work of art. Samuel L. Jackson, Frank Oz and Christopher Lee still do a very commendable and incredible job at their roles as Master Windu, Master Yoda and Count Dooku, all other actors were just as amazing that I can't even find the words. This film is so intriguing because it's exactly how we see one of the most iconic Disney villains ever come to be and if you ask me seeing how Darth Vader came to be is both very intriguing and pretty heartbreaking at the same time, but it's also still very intriguing as we see how Luke Skywalker and Princess Leia came to be as well. Watch this movie and see for yourself who the real culprit behind the war and the real Sith Lord really is, how Darth Vader came to be and what became of Luke and Leia after their birth, if you like it than excellent and keep on going because the excitement and surprises don't stop there, but if you don't like it than no worries because you can't please everyone. 10/10 For Sure!!!!!

Back to the Future
It's one of those rare films where nearly everything works.

Why it rocks: The "Back to the Future" trilogy is a timeless classic when it comes to memorable cinematic moments that have permanently shaped popular culture. This tale of time travel, friendship, and family captures the interest of viewers of all ages since it was expertly crafted and exhibited limitless inventiveness. As a seasoned author and movie enthusiast, I am eager to explore each chapter of this outstanding trilogy. With its brilliant premise and dynamic performers, "Back to the Future" hurled viewers into a thrilling adventure. Robert Zemeckis, the film's director, expertly combined sci-fi with uplifting storytelling to compel us to root for Marty McFly's (Michael J. Fox) quest to right the wrongs in the past while developing a special relationship with the quirky Doc Brown (Christopher Lloyd). The film's hilarious humour, catchy soundtrack, and dynamic pacing perfectly capture the vibe of the 1980s. I enjoy how the complex time-travel paradoxes are seamlessly woven together, creating a story that rewards attention to detail. This movie is still a showcase for inventive storytelling and the cinematic medium.
